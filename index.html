FAQ

Good to know

Are the tents air-conditioned?
Yes — every tent has A/C, a premium mattress and a private en-suite bathroom with a rainfall shower.
Is breakfast included?
Direct bookings include daily breakfast. Floating breakfast in the pool can be arranged on request.
How far is the beach?
Balangan Beach is about 5 minutes away; Dreamland and Bingin are within a 12-minute ride.
Do you offer airport transfers?
Yes — the airport is roughly 25 minutes away and we arrange transfers at a fixed price. Just message us on WhatsApp.
Is the retreat suitable for children?
Couples and solo travellers are our focus, but families with children are welcome — contact us to pick the right tent.
